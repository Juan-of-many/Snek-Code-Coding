Why Should You Care?

Origami is an accessible hobby available to people of all skill levels. Additionally, the art of paper-folding doesn't require any fancy materials, so it can be enjoyed across a variety of locations. Origami takes as little and as much time as you devote to it.

The origami community is super supportive! Someone starting out can find thousands of guides online and in books. It is something that can be enjoyed alone or with a group of friends. When you finish a piece, there are plenty of opportunities for you to share your creation with others.

From personal experience, Origami is genuinely enjoyable. I like working with my hands. The creative process of morphing paper into something recognizable leads to plenty of learning opportunities.

Be sure to check in again in the future for updated content. This site is a work-in-progress passion project!
