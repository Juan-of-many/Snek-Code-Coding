Looking Back & Looking Forward

Origami has been a hobby of mine for over a decade now.
Within every piece I fold, I place the energy of creation. I aim to touch the hearts of those around me. With just a few folds, something that would have otherwise been discarded turns into a thing of artistic beauty.

If it is discarded again, that is okay. It has already served its purpose. Even if it gave comfort or joy for only a small moment, that singular moment is forever worth the time it took to fold. In the end, one man's trash has literally become another's treasure.

I hope you've enjoyed this guide. It was made with some guidance and inspiration from wonderful friends, logo art by a dear friend Andromeda, and formatting help from W3schools guides.
